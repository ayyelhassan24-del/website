Initial qualification is a soft pull only. A hard inquiry only happens if you choose to accept a specific lender offer. You control that step completely.
How does zero percent interest actually work?
These are business credit line products with 0% promotional APR periods lasting 12 to 18 months. We structure the funding to maximize your zero percent window so you can deploy and generate returns before standard rates apply.
What if I have been denied before?
Bank denials are the most common situation we see. Traditional banks use rigid scoring boxes. Our 500+ lender network includes institutions that underwrite based on cash flow and business potential. A prior bank denial is not disqualifying here.
What are the minimum requirements?
Minimum $500K in annual business revenue, 6 months operating, personal credit 620 or above, and no open bankruptcies. Additional factors are reviewed case by case on the qualification call.
How fast is funding really?
Fastest we have seen is 24 hours after approval. Typical range is 5 to 11 business days depending on the lender and documentation speed. The qualification call gives you an accurate estimate based on your specific file.
What does this cost me?
